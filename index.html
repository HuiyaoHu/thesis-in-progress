—　TUTORIAL　—

1. Purchase Volume

Under Display Toggle, click on Storey 1 to turn on the working grid. Next, place Volume modules on the working grid to define the purchase area.

2. Module Placement

Step 1 : Place Floor modules in the purchase area to set up apartment floors and exterior walls.

　

Step 2 : Place Internal Wall modules on the Floor modules to configure the interior partitions.

　

Step 3 : Replace Internal Wall and Internal Wall modules with Hybrid Modules to add functional components in walls.

　

Step 4 : To build a multistorey apartment, click on other storeys under Display Toggle and repeat the process above. Stairs and Floor Openings can now be added.

　

—　CONTROLS　—
